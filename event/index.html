Special group events & retreats

Sonitori Hotel & Resort in Phnom Penh has extensive experience in organizing all types of events and customized activities for groups. From weddings to yoga, birthday or corporate retreats, family gatherings or even small conferences we let it happen exactly the way you want! We provide catering and can organize many other event related services such as the rental of sound systems, cultural or music performances, theme decorations, monks blessings, special lunches or dinners as well as fun activities like a tuktuk treasure hunt or a poolparty with Dj.

Pool party

Splash into style at Sonitori's tropical pool party! Sip cocktails by the saltwater pool under swaying palms. Chill vibes, upbeat music, and a lush green backdrop. Meet travelers, dance barefoot, and toast to golden sunsets. Float, laugh, and lounge in an urban oasis. Where Phnom Penh meets paradise—one splash at a time. Tropical bites and signature drinks served all afternoon. Perfect for friends, solo adventurers, and sunset seekers. This is more than a party—it's a Sonitori experience.
