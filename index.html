Sonitori Resort

The Sonitori Resort in Phnom Penh is an urban oasis located in the elegant BKK1 business center of Phnom Penh. in a city environment. The Resort is designed in a modern Asian style with all luxuries and the addition of locally crafted furniture, modular terrazzo surfaces, vibrant Asian art, Cambodian crafted silks, secluded balconies, alfresco private stone bathtubs. There is a salt-water pool with waterfall, pool-bar, restaurant and a Spa, all set within a lush tropical garden. Sonitori Resort – Phnom Penh caters for the independent traveler who wants some privacy and luxury, we are a gay friendly venture and are closely aligning with the local community.
