Activities

At Sonitori Resort Cambodia, each day invites you to explore, relax, and connect with the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Cambodia.

From Khmer cooking classes and guided tours of Angkor Wat and local temples, to rejuvenating spa treatments in our tropical garden setting, we offer unique and immersive activities for every type of traveler.

Guests can cycle through scenic routes, swim in our saltwater pool, or unwind poolside with a signature cocktail. Families will love our fun and safe children's programs, while couples and solo travelers can enjoy peaceful wellness moments.

As an LGBTQ+ friendly resort in Cambodia, Sonitori welcomes everyone into a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment. Whether you're visiting Phnom Penh, our team is here to help you plan meaningful experiences — both on-site and off.

Looking for local experiences? Click the guide on the right for more details on tours, local attractions, and exclusive hotel offerings curated just for you.

Speak with our friendly Sonitori team to discover the best things to do in Cambodia during your stay. From cultural adventures to relaxing getaways, Sonitori is your perfect base for unforgettable memories.
